Registration


PlistEdit Pro is a shareware application and requires Mac OS X 10.5.8 or later. You may try out PlistEdit Pro for a free trial period of 14 days. After that period, a reminder notice with a delay will appear each time you launch PlistEdit Pro. You must then purchase a license for PlistEdit Pro to avoid the startup delay.

PlistEdit Pro is $29.95 (US dollars) and can be purchased online with a credit card, debit card, or PayPal at our web store. If you wish to arrange alternate payment, please e-mail support@fatcatsoftware.com.
